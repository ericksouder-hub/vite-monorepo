## Design Context

### Users
B2B clients using Ava as an AI assistant to handle service scheduling and appointment booking on behalf of their businesses. Users expect efficiency, reliability, and a tool that feels like an extension of their team.

### Brand Personality
- **Sophisticated**: High-end B2B feel that commands trust.
- **Efficient**: Keyboard-first interactions and lightning-fast navigation.
- **Helpful but Unobtrusive**: The AI (Ava) should feel present but should never get in the user's way.
- **3-Word Personality**: Precise, Premium, Proactive.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Linear-Inspired**: Focus on subtle borders, high-quality dark mode, command-palette driven workflows, and a refined "studio" aesthetic.
- **Modern Tech**: Extensive use of OKLCH colors, Inter typography, and rounded cards (radius-lg/3xl).
- **Themes**: Full support for Light and Dark modes with high-contrast but readable components.

### Design Principles
1. **Keyboard-First Utility**: Every major action should be accessible via the command palette or shortcuts to maximize user productivity.
2. **Subtle Precision**: Use thin, consistent borders and high-precision spacing (matching the `radius-lg` standard) to create a clean, organized look.
3. **Intentional Motion**: Utilize `motion/react` for micro-interactions that provide feedback and feel fluid, never distracting.
4. **Data Density with Breathing Room**: Maintain high information density for business data, but balance it with consistent padding and intentional white space to prevent overwhelm.
